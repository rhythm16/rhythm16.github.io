# Quality resources I recommend

## Books
* The C Programming Language 2nd Ed. by Brian W. Kernighan, Dennis M. Ritchie\
"The Book" for C programming, less than 300 pages, clear and concise.\
* [Computer Systems: A Programmer's Perspective 3rd Ed. by Randal E. Bryand and David R. O'Hallaron](https://csapp.cs.cmu.edu)\
A thorough introduction on computer systems, it's also recommended by many others. I haven't gotten the time to read it, but the chapter on the "y86" CPU design seems interesting. Hope I can get to it sometime.
* Linux Device Driver 3rd Ed. by Jonathan Corbet, Alessandro Rubini and Greg Kroah-Hartman\
Another book praised by many people.
